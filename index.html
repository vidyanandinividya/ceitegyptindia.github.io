    About CEIT EGYPT-INDIA

    Centre for Excellence in Information Technology (CEIT) in Cairo, Egypt is the flagship programme of cutting-edge IT technology from India, under the auspices of India - Egypt Economic Cooperation. Based on the interest expressed by the Government of Egypt and the Government of India to jointly develop a Centre for Information and Communication Technology in Egypt, a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed between the two countries on 19th March 2013, for training Egyptian youth in specialized field of IT.
